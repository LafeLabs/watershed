<h2>Workflows</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Maps as service, hustle maps on Street for tourists</li>
    <li>Go out with GPS phone and survey, mark markers with GPS coordinates, note them down on paper</li>
    <li>Use a map program in a web browser without GPS to find locations of things, describe the thing/place, and note the coordinates in lat/lon format</li>
    <li>Wander, study, contemplate and choose a map coordinate and unit set: latlon0, latlon1, and unit</li>
    <li>Create a physical treasure map, laminated, hide treasure, find treasure</li>
    <li>Add links to a map to local businesses, talking to the owners physically as you go, to connect them to the map and each other</li>
    <li>Find sticks, whittle them, paint them, plant them as markers or distribute them</li>
    <li>Cut sticks to size, paint, mark, and drill for skeletron structures</li>
    <li>Create a shape table</li>
    <li>Create a font</li>
    <li>Edit main glyph of map</li>
    <li>Pollinators: walk from one marker in the physical map space to the next, moving objects and information around</li>
    <li>Sit at a node and wait for pollinators to come by, use them for distribution of the work you produce at that node/marker</li>
</ul>
