<h2>Tarot Cards</h2>
<p>These are not literally tarot cards.  However their size, shape, texture, look and feel and usage has enough in common with them to justify the name.  The "tarot" cards of Action Geometry, used throughout the Watershed system, are simply a means of communicating information consisting of cutout cards from cardstock.  Cards are generally about 3.5 inches by 5.5 inches.</p>
