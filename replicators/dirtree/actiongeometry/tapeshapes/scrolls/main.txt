<h2>Tape Shapes</h2>
<p>Tape Shapes are one of the simplest and easiest to replicate and also one of the most useful of the Action Geometry constructions.</p>
<p>If you are starting from scratch and have access to a printer but not an existing set of tapeshapes, you can print out the following svg files and cut them out.  If printed one to one in size, they should print to 3 inches on a side, assuming 100 px is 1 inch.</p>
